No. There are no contracts, no minimum spend requirements, and no exclusivity. You can stop using Affiliate Warehouse at any time with no penalties or notice period required.

You can compare Affiliate Warehouse pricing directly against your original trade accounts at any time. Transparent side-by-side comparison is a core feature. If Affiliate Warehouse pricing is not better, you have no obligation to use it.
